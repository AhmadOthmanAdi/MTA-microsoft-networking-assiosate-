🛎️IMPORTANT🛎️

Devices used in OSI layers :

  1. Physical layer or below : Hubs, Repeaters, Cables, Fibers, Wireless..
  1. Data-link layer: Bridges, Modems, Network cards, 2-layer switches.
  1. Network layer: Routers, Brouters, 3-layer switches.
  1. Transport layer: Gateways, Firewalls.
  1. Session layer: Gateways, Firewalls, PC’s.
  1. Presentation layer : Gateways, Firewalls, PC’s.
  1. Application layer: Gateways,Firewalls, all end devices like PC’s, Phones, Servers..
